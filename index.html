Let's add a recipe for some tasty fried Noodles

We're going to borrow a nice picture from Ada recipes

Here is what the recipe calls for

Ingredients (probably not right....)

  • 2 super pack indomie noodles
  • Water for cooking
  • A handful green beans
  • 2 small carrots
  • 1/2 medium green pepper
  • 1/2 medium onion
  • Powered curry enough for colouring
  • 1 small fresh pepper( This is optional because indomie comes with pepper, especially if it's prepared for kids)
  • 1 boiled egg
  • 2 cooking spoons vegetable oil

Instructions (may or may not be correct)

  1. Scrape, wash, chop carrot, green beans, onion, green and red pepper.
  2. In a boiling water, add curry powder and the noodles , cook for 7 minutes on medium heat.
  3. In an empty dry frying pan, put 2 cooking spoons vegetable oil, leave to heat, add the diced, onion, carrot, green beans, green and red pepper, stir-fry for 1 minutes
  4. Add the boiled indomie, fry for another 2 minutes and remove from heat.
  5. Your fried noodles is Ready!
  6. Get your forks, enjoy so delicious!
